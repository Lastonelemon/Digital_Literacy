I created my Digital Literacy Awareness infographic using Canva, a simple and user-friendly online design tool. I chose Canva because it provides a variety of templates, icons, and design elements that made it easy to create a visually appealing one-page infographic.

In my design, I focused on explaining the concept of digital literacy in a clear and engaging way. The infographic includes sections on what digital literacy means, useful digital tools for students such as Google Docs and PowerPoint, safe internet practices like using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links, and tips for maintaining a professional online presence. I also added a section on email etiquette to highlight the importance of proper communication in academic and professional settings.

While working on this infographic, I found it interesting how design choices like colors, icons, and layout can make information more attractive and easier to understand. However, I found it slightly challenging to include all the important points without making the design look too crowded. Overall, this task helped me improve both my digital skills and creativity.
